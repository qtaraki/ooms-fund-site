About OoMs

OoMs

The Order of Magnitudes (OoMs) is a visionary angel investment group dedicated to empowering early-stage technology companies that are redefining the limits of innovation.

More than an investment vehicle, OoMs represents a consortium of forward-thinking technologists, entrepreneurs, and investors committed to supporting breakthrough technologies—providing strategic mentorship, domain expertise, and a shared vision of progress beyond capital alone.

Our investment focus spans eight transformative domains:

  • Drones and Robotics — Transforming industries and defense with intelligent automation and precision systems.
  • Autonomous Vehicles — Leading the charge in mobility, revolutionizing transport and logistics.
  • Space Exploration — Fueling ventures that expand humanity's reach beyond Earth.
  • Post-Mobile Computing — Accelerating innovation in AR/VR, neural interfaces, and beyond.
  • Artificial Intelligence — Enabling smart systems that enhance decision-making and creativity.
  • Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain — Reinventing finance and trust through decentralized systems.
  • Quantum Computing — Unlocking unprecedented computational power for complex problem-solving.
  • Other forward-looking technologies — Investing in the unknown breakthroughs of tomorrow.

Participation in OoMs is by invite only. Please ask a member to learn more.
